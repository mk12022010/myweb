A Comprehensive Research Methodology Guide

From Theory to Practice

Author

Professor Dr. Md. Kamrul Hasan

“To ensure the validity and reproducibility of any research work, all actions must be justified with appropriate reasoning, for which Research Methodology serves as the only acceptable foundation.”
— M. K. Hasan

About This Book

This comprehensive guide covers all aspects of research methodology, from foundational concepts to advanced techniques. It serves as an essential resource for students, researchers, and academics across all disciplines.

How to Use This Book

This book is designed to be used both as a textbook for research methodology courses and as a reference guide for researchers. Each chapter builds upon previous concepts while remaining accessible as a standalone reference.
